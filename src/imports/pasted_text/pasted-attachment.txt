  }

The Effect: The element shrinks slightly on active (click) and returns to size.

.button:active {

Image Loading: Since it's a white UI, use aspect-ratio on your product images. This prevents "Layout Shift" (where the page jumps around as images load), which is the #1 killer of performance scores.

Why this is the "Pro" way:
Zero JS Dependency: All physics and animations are handled by the browser's engine.

Low Battery Impact: Because we aren't using a requestAnimationFrame loop, your site won't drain a user's phone battery.

Future Proof: scroll-snap and hover: hover are standard modern CSS.
